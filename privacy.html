Last updated: July 22, 2026

This privacy policy explains how WorldPulse ("we", "this site") handles visitor information. By using this site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

This is a static navigation website. We do not require registration and do not actively collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number. When you visit, the hosting service (GitHub Pages) automatically logs standard request data (IP address, access time, browser type) for security and operational purposes. We do not have access to or use these logs.

2. Cookies

This site itself does not set cookies. However, if third-party advertising or analytics services are integrated (see below), those providers may use cookies or similar technologies. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings.

3. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • GitHub Pages: Hosting service that stores and serves the website.
  • Hacker News API: Provides the live news feed. Your browser fetches data directly from their servers.
  • Counter API: Provides a simple page-view counter. It records anonymous visit counts only.
  • News outlets & search engines: Clicking a link or searching takes you to a third-party website governed by its own privacy policy.

  • You can manage your ad preferences at Google Ad Settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising from third-party vendors at aboutads.info.

5. Third-Party Links

This site contains many links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those external sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

7. Your Rights

Because this site does not actively collect personal data, there is typically no personal data to access or delete. If you have any questions about this policy, feel free to reach out.
